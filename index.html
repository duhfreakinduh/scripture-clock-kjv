The idea

Every minute points to Scripture.

The current hour becomes the chapter number, and the current minute becomes the verse number. At 3:16, the clock looks for a 3:16 reference—featuring John 3:16 as its signature reading.

Because not every possible time exists as a Bible reference, continuous mode uses a clearly labeled chapter-matched reading whenever an exact KJV reference is unavailable. It never invents a verse.

Scripture text: King James Version. Built as a quiet reminder to redeem the time.
